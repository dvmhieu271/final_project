Shopping

Diverse, well-invested shopping world that consists of stretchy rows of souvenir shops at train stations and the French Village quarter will enable visitors to easily cherry pick souvenirs following their vacations. Or else, visitors can also roam the shopping mall and marvel at the genuine French architecture, draped in a heavenly world blessed with soothing tranquility and total isolation from the outside buzz.

Welcome to Ba Na Hills, where all the fun begin!
