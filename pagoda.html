Linh Phong Zen Monastery

Linh Phong Zen Monastery (alternatively known as the Northern Pagoda) is a quintessential reenactment of the Northern Buddhist architecture and totally constructed out of Erythrophleum fordii timber. Situated in the southeast, Linh Phong Zen Monastery inclides the Front Hall, Rear Hall and gardens. In its compound lie statue of Buddha and 18 Alahats standing in two left and right rows.
