Tickets

How can I buy tickets?

– Direct purchases at automatic ticket booths and kiosks on Ba Na Hills

– Online booking via website https://ticket.sunworld.vn

– Purchase through travel agencies

What are the ticket fares of Sun World Ba Na Hills?

– Ticket fare of Ba Na Hills for visitors:

    • VND700,000/grown-up

    • VND550,000/child (1m – 1.4m in height)

– Ticket fare for Danang locals:

    • VND400,000/grown-up

    • VND300,000/child (1m – 1.4m in height)

– Children who stand over 1.4m are subject to grown-up fare. Children below 1m in height are exempt from ticket fare.

Note:

– Fares include entry ticket of the complex

– Fares include 10% of VAT

– Fares include most games in our Fantasy Park: 105 games and the Legendary Knight (tube slide); Hiking train to Debay Wine Cellar and entry to Le Jardin D’Amour gardens, Golden Bridge.

– Fares exclude entry to the Wax Museum: VND100,000/grown-up, Teddy bear grips and Fantasy Park indoor sports.

How to use online tickets bought via website ticket.sunworld.vn?

– After successfully booking and paying ticket via wesite ticket.sunworld.vn, the system will automatically send digital ticket(s) to your email. At the entry, you just present the digital ticket(s) to checkers at the checkpoint.

What is the business course of Ba Na cable cars?

– Ba Na cable cars are operated on five routes: Suoi Mo – Ba Na, Debay – Morin, Toc Tien Waterfall – L’Indochine, Hoi An – Marseille and Bordeaux – Louvre. Capacity 6,000 passengers/hour and open between 7.30am and 9pm on all weekdays.

Service

Is there any harmful health effect of such glaring height differences? Who are not recommended for cable cars?

– Given great height differences, low air pressure, diluted air and diminishing oxygen density, visitors should walk slow and keep breathing slowly to keep your health in check. Visitors with cardiovascular diseases, elevated blood pressure, respiratory diseases, etc., pregnant women and children should consider their health status before taking a cable car.

Commuting - Transport

Does the complex offer any shuttle buses for visitors?

At the moment, Sun World Ba Na Hills doesn’t offer pick-up services during daytime. Pick-up services are only available for guests using “Night Combo” (visiting Ba Na after 16:00 daily).

How long does it take to commute from the downtown of Danang City to Sun World Ba Na Hills?

It takes visitors who travel by bus round half an hour to commute from the downtown of Danang City to Sun World Ba Na Hills.
